Collectify Pro: For Every Collector

January 2025 · 14 min read · SwiftUI & Barcode APIs
I’m a video game collector. After years of buying games, I had no clear idea what my collection was actually worth — or which items were the real gems. So I built Collectify Pro. Not just for me, but for every collector.

The Origin

At some point, I looked at my shelves and asked myself:

“How much did I actually spend on all this — and what is it worth today?”

I tried spreadsheets. I tried existing apps. None of them stuck.

The Core Idea

A universal, private, and free app to catalog anything you collect.

  • Works for any type of collection
  • No subscriptions
  • No accounts
  • All data stored locally
  • Barcode scanning to save time

Key Features

Barcode Scanning

Instantly fetch product data from large barcode databases.

Manual Entry

Full control over photos, notes, condition, and metadata.

Value Tracking

Track purchase price versus current market value.

Rarity Detection

Automatically highlights your most valuable items.

Export

CSV, PDF, and Excel exports for insurance and backups.

Privacy First

No cloud sync, no accounts, no data collection.

Tech Stack

Swift SwiftUI Core Data Vision Barcode APIs PDFKit iOS 18+

What I Learned

  • Build something you personally need
  • One killer feature beats ten average ones
  • Privacy is a real selling point
  • Data export builds user trust
