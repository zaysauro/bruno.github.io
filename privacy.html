Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications developed and published by Bruno Zayed. Each app respects your privacy and complies with Apple’s App Store Guidelines. My apps are designed to operate transparently and securely, prioritizing user privacy and data protection.

1. Limitaê

Limitaê does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All financial and usage data entered by the user is stored locally on the user’s iPhone using Apple’s secure storage systems. The app functions 100% offline and does not communicate with any external servers or third-party APIs.

2. CoffeePixelApp

CoffeePixelApp also does not collect or share any user data. All image processing and pixel-art transformations occur locally on the user’s device. No photos, personal information, or analytics are transmitted or stored externally.

3. Freqly – English → Japanese Frequency Dictionary

Freqly is a fully offline English → Japanese frequency dictionary. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind.

Data Collection

The app does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Identifiers
  • Device information

Offline Operation

Freqly works entirely offline. All vocabulary and features are available without an internet connection. No information is transmitted to any server.

Favorites & Local Data

Any user preferences or favorites are stored locally on the device and never leave the user’s iPhone.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or external APIs.

4. Daily Psalms & Bible

Daily Psalms & Bible is a Christian devotional app that allows users to read Psalms, Bible verses, and daily spiritual reflections. The app is designed to be simple, inspiring, and distraction-free.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No personal information
  • No analytics or tracking by the developer
  • No account or login required
  • No cloud storage or external servers

Offline Usage

Daily Psalms & Bible works fully offline after installation. Scripture content is displayed locally on the device and does not require a network connection.

Advertising & Monetization

The app may display banner ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous data required for ad delivery, following Google’s privacy policies. No data is collected by the developer.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob for advertising
  • No other third-party SDKs are used

User Content

The app does not allow users to upload, generate, or share content. There is no cloud sync, account system, or remote storage.

5. General Privacy Principles

  • All apps are built with user privacy as a core principle.
  • No user data is sold, shared, or analyzed by external entities.
  • All storage is local unless explicitly stated otherwise in a future app’s description.

6. Third-Party Services

At this time, none of the listed apps use external APIs or cloud services for analytics or personal data collection. Any future apps that use services such as CloudKit or Firebase will have their usage clearly disclosed in updated versions of this policy.
